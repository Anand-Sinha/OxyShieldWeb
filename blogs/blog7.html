Valved vs Valveless facemasks

Valved vs Valveless facemasks

When searching for masks online, valved masks will often make an appearance in your search results. These masks are often chosen for two supposed reasons- they provide better ventilation and prevent moisture buildup. However, in terms of the COVID-19 pandemic, valved masks are beneficial to no one but the wearer of the masks. In the long term, even the wearer cannot protect themselves from a pandemic whose numbers are increasing due to people wearing the wrong type of mask.

Let us understand the mechanism behind the use of the valved masks. Valved N95 masks are popularly used in the process of making heavy arts which involve drilling or sanding, sweeping areas with heavy dust, removing mold and so on. The basic function of the mask is to prevent toxins from reaching the wearer itself.

However, in the midst of a global pandemic, the goal is not only to protect oneself, but all those around us. One way valve masks filter the air going in- preventing potential toxic airborne particles from affecting the wearer of the mask. When the air is exhaled, it is not filtered- thus, it leaves with water vapor, oxygen particles, and even Corona Virus particles. The valve acts as a comfort device. As mentioned in How N95 Masks Work, the masks need to form an airtight seal around the mouth and nose in order to function to the best of their ability. The valves, thus, act as a breach in the airtight seal around the bottom half of the face. Yes, one can agree that it provides relief in terms of humidity and ease of breath- however, in the long term, the improper use of the valved N95 masks can only lead to a rise in the number of recorded cases.

Primarily healthcare workers used valve-less masks- pre-COVID-19. In current times, they have been adopted by the general public as well- for good reason. The valve-less masks help in creating a safe and sanitary space in public, at home, and- most importantly- in hospitals. Along with frequent washing of hands and social distancing, valve-less masks are essential in helping curb the rising numbers of the Coronavirus pandemic. In areas where social distancing is difficult to maintain, valve-less masks are your best friends- wearing them shows concern for your health, along with the health of those around you.
