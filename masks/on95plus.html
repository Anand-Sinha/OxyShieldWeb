What is an N-95 Respirator?



N95 masks or N95 respirators re examples of Personal protective Equipment (PPE’s). N95 is a classification or masks and filters. N95 masks filter out 95% of airborne particles such as viruses, bacteria, pollution and help prevent diseases.

N95 is a classification of the U.S. NIOSH for air filtration. N95 respirators are functionally equivalent to FFP2 respirators of the European Union and KN95 respirators of China.

We make CE and FDA certified FFP2 Respirators which are superior, safer, and reliable.

Currently N95 respirators are the recommended equipment for doctors, healthcare practitioners and others on the front-line battling coronavirus (COVID-19) by the FDA and other medical authorities
