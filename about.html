About.

We are a mask manufacturing company in Chhattisgarh, India dedicated to creating the best quality products

OxyShield is a technology first, quality company aiming to set new standards in the industry with our hygiene, efficiency and humanity

OxyShield created a unique facemask to bring design and reusability to highly efficient filtering.

Our mission has never changed: to give the world a mask that makes you look great while providing the protection from airborne particles

OxyShield supports clean air and humanitarian efforts around the world. We seek to improve quality of life and instill civil responsibility that has a direct impact on society and the environment

Our Mission

Build the best product, cause no unnecessary harm, use business to inspire and implement solutions to health crisis.

Our Vision

To become the benchmark manufacturing company for the PPE industry providing world-class products. To create a better everyday life for many people.

The OxyShield Team

We have committed ourselves to do our part in preparing the people with the essential tools to safeguard themselves from germs and disease by ensuring the best quality respirators. We have pledged to offer better quality at fair prices and to set a new benchmark in the emerging Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) industry in India.

Confidence, Trust, Reliability.

OxyShield is a subsidiary owned by Phil Group, a ₹4 Billion company in manufacturing and processing of minerals. We are committed to break the barriers of standard in the Indian industry with the best, state of art facility and pharmaceutical grade hygienic production practises.

Our industry is equipped with high end machinery capable of producing 500,000 particulate respirators every day. In addition to technology and quality, we have a management with over 100 years of cumulative experience in safety equipment manufacturing and heavy industries. Combining the two, we aim to allow our products’ users a sanitised lifestyle, free from fear and worry.

At a time of crisis such as the COVID-19 pandemic, we channel our resources in order to provide the world and it’s frontline warriors with essential equipment to stay healthy and avoid bacterial/viral infections and diseases.

We strongly recommend you to buy such crucial protective equipment from a genuine and approved manufacturer. Always ask for a video of the manufacturing facility and copy of lab reports before purchasing such equipment.
