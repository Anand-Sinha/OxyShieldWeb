Wellness

In certain circumstances, each of us has thought, "I shouldn't be breathing this." The quality of air we breathe contributes directly to our health. OxyShield face masks offers protection from microscopic particles resulting from air pollution, dust, smoke, allergens, biologics, and natural causes. Wellness results from conscientiously applied healthy living principles and starts with breathing clean air. OxyShield face masks is efficient for helping to protect from viral and bacterial airborne particles, fitted properly.

Poor Air Quality

OxyShield face masks is widely used in urban environments due to the high levels of air pollution. Particulate matter (PM) is classified based on the size of particle diameter.

Inhalable-Particles of 100 microns diameter or less.

Thoracic-Particles of 10 microns diameter and less that will reach the lungs. Referred to as PM10.

Respirable-Particles that will penetrate into the gas exchange region of the lungs. Particle sizes of 2.5 micron are referred to as PM2.5 and considered hazardous.

OxyShield face masks helps to protect from inhaled particles of mean diameter 0.3 microns.

Allergies

Pollen particles entering the respiratory system often measure from 3 microns to 100 microns. We are currently experiencing an increase in sensitivity to allergens. Wearing OxyShield face masks is a non invasive way to protect your lungs from inhalation of allergens when pollen count is high and in environments of triggering particles and odors.

Travel

Whether you are travelling by air, train, bicycle, car, or just walking around, OxyShield face masks provides highly efficient protection from airborne contaminants. Our stylish designs present a friendly face that inspires confidence and promotes health wherever you travel. Start a conversation about clean air by wearing a OxyShield face masks.

Sensetive Lungs

There are many reasons that people wear a mask to protect their lungs. Some suffer from respiratory ailments such as asthma, some have suppressed or recovering immune systems. Highly efficient filtering masks help to protect sensitive lungs from microscopic particles.

Outdoor

OxyShield face masks is highly efficient for keeping known hazardous particles out of the respiratory system especially in dusty, moldy, polluted, post-combustion, high PM, high pollen, wildfire, volcanic particulate matter, and crowded environments.
